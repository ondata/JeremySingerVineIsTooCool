About this project

Data Is Plural is like a mystery box for data junkies: once a week Jeremy shares new five datasets about many different topics via newsletter, and we love him for that, but mostly we love that he has archived every link he sends in a Google spreadsheet that we have used to build this page.

We are passionate about making things work and we hope that other fans of Jeremy's newsletter will appreciate our tool.

Jeremy

Jeremy Singer-Vine (@jsvine) lives in New York and works at BuzzFeed as data editor.

He's a journalist and a computer programmer. He publishes Data Is Plural, a weekly newsletter of useful/curious datasets, and co-organizes the Hacks/Hackers NYC meetup. He had worked at The Wall Street Journal and, before that, at Slate Magazine.
